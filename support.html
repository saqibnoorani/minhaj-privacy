Frequently Asked Questions

How are prayer times calculated?

Minhaj uses the Aladhan API with your location to calculate prayer times based on established Islamic calculation methods. You can change the calculation method in Settings.

Why does the app need my location?

Location is used to calculate accurate prayer times and Qibla direction for your area. The app only requests approximate (coarse) location and never stores it on any server.

Does the app work offline?

Most features work offline, including dhikr tracking, duas, worship guides, and previously loaded Quran pages. Prayer times require an initial network fetch but are cached for offline use.

How do I reset my progress/data?

You can reset all data by uninstalling and reinstalling the app. All data is stored locally on your device.

Why does the app need camera access?

Camera access is only used for the Qibla AR feature, which overlays the Qibla direction on your camera view. You can use the standard compass instead if you prefer.

Why does the app need microphone access?

Microphone access is only for the Arabic pronunciation practice feature. Audio is processed entirely on your device and is never recorded or sent anywhere.

Do you collect any personal data?

No. All your data stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no tracking. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

The app is not showing prayer times correctly.

Make sure location permission is granted (Settings > Minhaj > Location). Also verify you've selected the correct calculation method for your region in the app's prayer settings.
