Privacy Policy

Minhaj (منهاج) — Islamic Companion App

Last updated: April 5, 2026

Overview

Minhaj is built with privacy as a core principle. We do not track you, we do not serve ads, and we do not sell your data. The app collects only the minimum data needed to provide its features, and almost everything stays on your device.

Data We Collect

Location (Coarse)

When you grant permission, Minhaj accesses your approximate location to:

  • Calculate accurate prayer times for your area
  • Determine the Qibla direction
  • Display local weather information

Location is requested with reduced accuracy (kilometer-level). It is never stored on any server or linked to your identity.

Camera & Microphone

  • Camera is used only for the Qibla AR (augmented reality) feature. No images are captured, stored, or transmitted.
  • Microphone is used only for Arabic pronunciation practice. Audio is processed entirely on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. No audio is sent to any server.

Third-Party Services

Minhaj communicates with the following public APIs to provide core features:

  • Aladhan API (api.aladhan.com) — receives your latitude/longitude to return prayer times. Aladhan Privacy Policy
  • Open-Meteo API (api.open-meteo.com) — receives your latitude/longitude to return weather data. Open-Meteo is a privacy-respecting, open-source weather API with no tracking.
  • Al Quran Cloud API (api.alquran.cloud) — provides Quran text and audio. No personal data is sent.
  • Quran.com API (api.quran.com) — provides word-by-word data. No personal data is sent.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs are integrated into the app.

Data Stored on Your Device

All personal data (prayer tracking, dhikr counts, reading progress, bookmarks, notes, and settings) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard storage. This data is not transmitted anywhere.

Widgets & Live Activities

Minhaj offers home screen widgets and Live Activities for prayer times. Data is shared between the app and widgets using Apple's App Groups, which keeps it sandboxed on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your personal data to any third party for marketing, advertising, or analytics purposes.

Children's Privacy

Minhaj does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is educational in nature and contains no objectionable content.

Your Rights

Since all your data is stored locally on your device, you have full control. You can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its data in iOS Settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
